Software Engineer
--------------------------

Six months ago, I traded steel beams and crane controls for JavaScript and component trees. After years in the oilfield and construction industry — including time as a crane operator — I decided to pivot into frontend development after suffering a back injury. What started as curiosity quickly became obsession: I now architect modular, mobile-first UIs with scoped logic and physics-based interactions.

I thrive on explicit control, clean code, and strategic troubleshooting. Whether I'm refining recoil FX in a Gravity Gun demo or benchmarking layout hydration across devices, I bring the same grit and precision I used on job sites to every line of code. My goal is to build interfaces that feel kinetic, intuitive, and future-proof — no overengineering, no assumptions.

I'm currently building rhythm-mapped lottery simulations and mobile-only physics handlers, and I'm open to collaborating on projects that push boundaries while respecting clean architecture.
